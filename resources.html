Clarinet and Saxophone Reeds

Clarinet and saxophone reeds have a numbering system for how "hard" they are to blow. You might need to try a few different levels to find what is best for you and it will change overtime and with experience so don't overbuy. Younger children beginners will probably need a 1 reed, which is very soft. Children who been playing for a while or older (and stronger) beginners, I would suggest a 2 or 2.5 reed. Intermediates and advanced players will usually play on anything from a 2.5 to a 3.5. To make matters a little more confusing, all brands are not made equal so you might love a 2.5 on one brand and a 3 on another.
